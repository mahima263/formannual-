Annual Giving is how the IIT Kanpur community comes together. Alumni, faculty, friends, and well-wishers to strengthen the Institute we all believe in. Every contribution supports students, empowers faculty, and advances the ideas and innovations that define IIT Kanpur.

Last year marked a bold step towards strengthening IIT Kanpur's global impact. This progress is made possible by the commitment and support of the IITK community.

When you give annually, you don't just support a cause you sustain momentum. With each gift, you become an active partner in shaping the Institute's future and carrying its legacy forward.

Stories of Change

A Timely Gift, A Stronger Future

Coming from a small farming family in rural Uttar Pradesh, Anshika knew early what financial uncertainty felt like. While a tuition fee waiver helped her enter IIT Kanpur, daily expenses like mess bills, electricity charges, and a failing laptop became overwhelming. At a critical moment, alumni support arrived. Pending dues were cleared, her laptop was repaired, and for the first time in months, Anshika could focus fully on her academics and placement preparation. This timely support restored not just stability, but confidence. Inspired by alumni generosity, she now dreams of giving back just as she was supported.

Anshika Singh
B.Tech, IITK Student

From Campus to Global Champions

When Team Aerial Robotics qualified for the global finals at ICUAS 2025 in the United States, travel costs stood between them and the world stage. Alumni stepped in. With support from the IITK Alumni Association (Outer Delhi Chapter), the team represented IIT Kanpur internationally and returned as global winners. Competing against top teams from across continents, their first-place finish showcased what becomes possible when alumni invest in student ambition. This victory is a powerful reminder: alumni support doesn't just fund travel, it fuels global excellence and institutional pride.

Team Aerial Robotics
Winners ICUAS 2025

Empowering Ability Through Giving

Gaurav Swarnkar's journey in para-athletics is defined by determination and strengthened by support. With financial assistance from the Cell for Differently Abled Persons (CDAP), Gaurav accessed training and competition opportunities that transformed effort into achievement. His medals at state and national para-athletics championships stand as proof of what targeted support can unlock. Alumni and institutional backing helped convert talent into podium finishes, reinforcing a powerful truth: when barriers are removed, potential rises. Gaurav's success reflects the lasting impact of inclusive and purpose-driven giving.

Gaurav Swarnkar
Winner Para Athletics 2025

Voices of Generosity

Mr. Abhay K. Bhushan
B.Tech/EE/1965
Chairman, Asquare, Inc.

Being from the first batch of IIT Kanpur, filled me with hope and a can-do spirit which helped me in developing the Internet and I am grateful to IIT Kanpur for this and I want to continue giving back, both individually and collectively. I encourage all alumni to give back. TOGETHER WE CAN!

Mr. Dhanraj K. Chokappa
(BT/CHE/1982),
Independent Consultant. Retired from Unilever R&D Bangalore

My five years as a BTech student from 1977 to 1982 provided a strong foundation for my subsequent achievements. One enduring memory is of my roommate during the first three years, who, despite coming from a humble rural background, cleared the JEE through his own efforts. Fortunately, tuition and hostel fees were very nominal at that time. With a merit cum means scholarship, he graduated with good grades and is now well-settled abroad. Today, tuition fees and other expenses are beyond the reach of many deserving students. Helping such students is my primary motivation to give back to IITK.
